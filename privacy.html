# Privacy Policy for AdMonitor **Last Updated:** April 13, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how AdMonitor ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the AdMonitor iOS application (the "App"). **1. Data Collection and Usage** AdMonitor is designed to act as a client-side dashboard to view your personal Google AdMob data. To provide this functionality, the App requests permission to log in to your Google Account. The App requests the following Google API scopes: - **Google Sign-In:** To authenticate your identity securely. - **Google AdMob API (`admob.readonly`):** To securely fetch your AdMob dashboard metrics (such as revenue, impressions, and clicks) and display them within the App. **2. Where is Your Data Stored?** All data retrieved from your Google Account (including OAuth tokens, AdMob statistics, and profile information) is: - Stored locally and securely on your own device. - Optionally backed up to your personal, private Apple iCloud account (to persist your data across your devices). - Never transmitted to us, the developers of AdMonitor. - Placed strictly under your control. **3. Third-Party Services** The App uses Google’s APIs to function. By logging in to your Google Account via our App, your authentication is handled entirely by Google, subject to Google's own Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties whatsoever. **4. Data Retention and Deletion** Because we do not store your data on our servers, we have no data to delete on your behalf. You can revoke AdMonitor’s access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account settings (Security > Third-party apps with account access) and removing AdMonitor. Furthermore, you can delete all your cached data simply by uninstalling the App from your device. **5. Changes to This Privacy Policy** We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. **6.
